### Lesson 2.1: Viral Content Formulas

**Formula 1: The Contrarian Take**

"Everyone says [common belief], but actually [opposite is true]. Here's why: [3 reasons]"

Example: "Everyone says you need a huge email list to make money online. Actually, 1,000 engaged subscribers beats 100,000 disengaged ones. Here's why..."

**Formula 2: The List**

"[NUMBER] [ADJECTIVE] [NOUNS] that [BENEFIT]"

Example: "5 AI tools that save me 20 hours per week"

**Formula 3: The Question**

"What if [SCENARIO]? [ANSWER]. Here's how..."

Example: "What if you could automate 80% of your work? You could. Here's how..."

**Formula 4: The Story**

"I was [SITUATION]. Then [TURNING POINT]. Now [RESULT]. Here's what I learned..."

Example: "I was broke with no business. Then I built a $1M company. Now I help others do the same. Here's what I learned..."

**Formula 5: The Data**

"[NUMBER]% of people [BEHAVIOR]. This is why [REASON]. The solution: [SOLUTION]"

Example: "92% of people abandon their goals by January 15th. This is why most people fail at New Year's resolutions. The solution: focus on systems, not goals."
