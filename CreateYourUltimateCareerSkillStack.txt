## ❓How To Use This Prompt:

● Replace the placeholders ([TARGET INDUSTRY], [CURRENT SKILLS], [EXPERIENCE LEVEL], [CAREER GOALS]) with your real info and goals.

● Example:

- Target industry: “Technology”
- Skills: “Python, video editing, SEO”
- Experience: “Intermediate”
- Career goals: “Become a senior content strategist focused on multimedia content”
