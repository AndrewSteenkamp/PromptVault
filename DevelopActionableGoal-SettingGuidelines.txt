## 💡Additional Tips:

● Use the SMART framework (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) as a foundation for your principles.

● Break down big stretch goals into smaller sub-goals to maintain momentum and reduce overwhelm.

● Align stretch goals with core values and interests to maximize intrinsic motivation and commitment.

● Monitor progress regularly and celebrate both small and large milestones to keep motivation high.

● Build in accountability by sharing goals and progress with a peer, coach, or mentor for support and encouragement.
