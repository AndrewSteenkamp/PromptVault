### Video Organization Tips

**Suggested Video Structure:**
1. **Introduction to Dropshipping** (Order: 0)
2. **Finding Profitable Products** (Order: 1)
3. **Setting Up Your Store** (Order: 2)
4. **Supplier Management** (Order: 3)
5. **Marketing Strategies** (Order: 4)
6. **Scaling Your Business** (Order: 5)

**For Each Video:**
- **Title**: Clear, descriptive name
- **Description**: Brief summary of what students will learn
- **Order Index**: Number determining video sequence (0, 1, 2, etc.)
- **Duration**: Automatically detected when uploaded

### Video Requirements

**Supported Formats:**
- MP4 (recommended)
- AVI, MOV, WMV, FLV, WebM

**Recommended Settings:**
- Resolution: 1080p or 720p
- Format: MP4 with H.264 encoding
- Audio: AAC encoding
- File size: Under 500MB per video (for faster uploads)

### Security Features
