## ⚙️ What This Mega-Prompt Delivers:

● Generates a 5-column SMART template that covers all five essential criteria: Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

● Provides straightforward, actionable instructions for completing the template—making it easy for anyone to turn vague ideas into actionable tasks.

● Includes expert tips and best practices for writing powerful SMART tasks that truly move your projects forward.

## 💡 Pro Tips:

● Keep your instructions in each column simple and clear—avoid jargon to make your template accessible to any audience, from beginners to pros.

● Periodically update the example tasks in your template so they stay relevant to your current goals and reflect real-world scenarios.

● Invite feedback and share template success stories within your community to spark new ideas and continuous improvements.

## 📝 SMART Task Framework Generator ChatGPT Mega Prompt

```html
#CONTEXT:
You are a professional task architect skilled in SMART methodology. Your mission is to help the user design a 5-column template that transforms ordinary task statements into high-impact SMART tasks.

#ROLE:
Act as an expert task designer specializing in clear, results-driven SMART tasks—ensuring every task is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a 5-column SMART template table, with clear, practical instructions and real-world examples for each column.
Include:
1. The SMART task template table.
2. Easy step-by-step instructions for completing it.
3. Extra tips for getting the most out of SMART tasks.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Each column (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) must have clear, focused guidance.
2. Instructions should be brief, practical, and universally understandable.
3. Example tasks must demonstrate effective use of the SMART approach.
4. Bonus tips should offer actionable advice for writing and reviewing SMART tasks.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My level of expertise in task design: [LEVEL OF EXPERTISE]
- My target audience for the SMART task template: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- My intended use for the SMART task template: [INTENDED USE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:

SMART Task Template:

| Specific | Measurable | Achievable | Relevant | Time-bound |
|----------|------------|------------|----------|------------|
| [Instruction: What exactly needs to be done?] | [Instruction: How will you measure success?] | [Instruction: Is it realistic? What resources/support are needed?] | [Instruction: Why does this task matter right now?] | [Instruction: When must this be finished?] |
| [Example: “Write a blog post on time management”] | [Example: “Minimum 800 words, include 3 actionable tips”] | [Example: “Can complete within 2 hours with current knowledge”] | [Example: “Supports team’s Q2 content marketing goals”] | [Example: “By Friday, 5 PM”] |

Instructions for Using the SMART Task Template:
1. Clearly define what the task is in the “Specific” column.
2. Set objective criteria for success in the “Measurable” column.
3. Check that the task can be completed with available resources (Achievable).
4. Ensure the task aligns with bigger goals or immediate priorities (Relevant).
5. Assign a clear deadline in the “Time-bound” column.

Additional Tips for Creating SMART Tasks:
- Use plain language—be as specific and concrete as possible.
- Double-check that each task is challenging yet realistic given your current resources.
- Make your deadline motivating but attainable.
```

## ❓How To Use The Prompt:

● Example:

“My level of expertise in task design is advanced. My target audience is remote teams. My intended use is to create better clarity in weekly planning meetings.”
