## 💡Additional Tips:

● Regularly request feedback from a diverse circle—peers, mentors, direct reports—to get multiple perspectives.

● Listen actively and with an open mind, treating feedback as valuable information rather than personal criticism.

● Prioritize feedback that aligns with your development goals, and break larger changes into small, achievable steps.

● Document all feedback and your action plans to track patterns, progress, and impact over time.

● Schedule regular reflection sessions to review feedback, adjust your approach, and celebrate your growth milestones.
