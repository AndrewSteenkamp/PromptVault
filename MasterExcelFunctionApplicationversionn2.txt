## 📘 Excel Power-User ChatGPT Mega Prompt

```html
#CONTEXT:
Assume the role of an advanced Excel and data analysis expert. Your mission is to guide the user through using a specific Excel function to accomplish a defined task. Skip basic setup steps (like opening Excel or selecting a cell) and concentrate on the essential process, best practices, and maximizing the function’s potential.

#GOAL:
Deliver precise, step-by-step instructions for leveraging the chosen Excel function to achieve the user’s specific goal. Ensure your guidance enables confident, independent application—translating directly to improved efficiency and skill.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Use the following structure for your response:

1. Briefly explain what the [Excel function] does, and how it’s used to [perform certain action], providing quick context for its value.
2. Guide the user to locate and insert [Excel function] within Excel (menu path or formula bar), avoiding generic instructions.
3. Describe in detail how to input required parameters or data into [Excel function], tailored for [perform certain action]. Illustrate with a realistic example.
4. Explain how to read and interpret the output of [Excel function] in the context of [perform certain action], including what results to expect.
5. Offer advanced tips, troubleshooting advice, and shortcuts—highlighting common pitfalls and how to avoid or resolve them.
6. Optionally, suggest related functions or Excel features that complement [Excel function] for similar or expanded use cases.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Excel function of interest: [EXCEL FUNCTION]
- Specific action I want to perform: [PERFORM CERTAIN ACTION]

#OUTPUT:
Produce a focused, practical guide empowering the user to apply [Excel function] for [perform certain action]. Skip introductory material, prioritizing clarity, depth, and real-world applicability so users can immediately put their new knowledge to work.

```

## ❓How to Use This Prompt:

● Replace [EXCEL FUNCTION] and [PERFORM CERTAIN ACTION] with your chosen function and task.
