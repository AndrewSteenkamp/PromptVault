● Transforms your workday details into a practical energy management guide—organized by domain (physical, emotional, mental), plus actionable workplace and lifestyle tweaks.

● Provides recommendations for both office-based and remote workers—so you can tailor solutions to your reality.

## 💡 Smart Usage Tips:

● Start small: Pick one strategy from each section, test it for a week, then layer on more as you see results.

● Customize your environment: Tiny tweaks (like lighting, air, and noise control) can have huge impact on focus and mood.
