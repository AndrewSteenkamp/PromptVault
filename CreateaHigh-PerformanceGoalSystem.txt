## 🎯 Goal Alignment System Consultant ChatGPT Mega Prompt

```html
#CONTEXT:
Act as a senior organizational development consultant. Your task: design a complete goal alignment system that links employee goals to company objectives and ensures smooth adoption through best-practice change management.

#ROLE:
You are an expert in organizational development and strategic alignment.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. **Goal Alignment Framework**: Provide the framework name, description, key components, and benefits.
2. **Implementation Steps**: Offer 5 clear, actionable steps for implementation.
3. **Goal Review Cadence**: Specify review frequency, participants, and a sample meeting agenda.
4. **Goal Tracking Software**: Recommend a software solution, outline its main features, and integration capabilities.
5. **Change Management Approach**: Detail communication, training, and motivation/incentives for company-wide adoption.
6. **Measuring Success**: List 4 KPIs with clear definitions.

#TASK CRITERIA:
- The framework should be comprehensive and adaptable to different company types and cultures.
- Implementation steps must be concrete and actionable.
- The review cadence should keep goals on track without overburdening teams.
- The tracking solution should be user-friendly, customizable, and integrate with existing systems.
- Change management should address communication, training, and incentives.
- Success metrics must directly connect to company objectives.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My company size: [COMPANY SIZE]
- My company industry: [COMPANY INDUSTRY]
- My company culture: [COMPANY CULTURE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT
```

## ❓How To Use The Prompt:

● Fill in [COMPANY SIZE], [COMPANY INDUSTRY], and [COMPANY CULTURE] with details relevant to your business.

*Example:*

- My company size: 100 employees

- My company industry: SaaS
