## ❓How to Use This Prompt:

● Replace [DESCRIBE YOUR ISSUE TO SOLVE] with a detailed summary of your specific Excel problem (data layout, sample data, your objective, and what you’ve tried so far).

## 📥 Example Input:

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
